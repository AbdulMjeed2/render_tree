Why Plant Trees?

🌡️

Cool Our Cities

Trees reduce urban temperatures by up to 8°C, making our cities more comfortable to live in.

💨

Clean Air

Each tree absorbs harmful pollutants and produces fresh oxygen for our communities.

💧

Conserve Water

Trees help prevent soil erosion and improve water quality in our environment.

🦋

Support Wildlife

Trees provide habitat for birds, insects, and other wildlife, creating thriving ecosystems.
