Our Mission

Green Saudi is a high school extracurricular club dedicated to empowering students to make a positive environmental impact through tree planting and environmental awareness. We believe that every student can contribute to creating a greener, more sustainable future for Saudi Arabia.

Our Vision

We envision a Saudi Arabia where high school students lead the way in environmental conservation, where every young person contributes to tree planting initiatives, and where our generation sets an example for future generations to follow.
